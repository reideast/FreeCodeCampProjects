Bill Gates
Creating a New Epoch for Humanity

Bill Gates is one of the most recogniziable figures of the modern microcomputer age. He co-founded Microsoft in 1975, and helped guide the company into a global powerhouse. His ideas in competition with other similar companies created a new way for people to use computers, and have surred a new era of widespread acceptance of computers for more people than ever before.

After retiring from Microsoft, Gates has gone on to found the Gates Foundation with his wife, Melinda. The foundation seeks to combat global issues, like poverty and education, and want to solve all the problems, even the hard ones, and have th whole world working together. Bill Gate's personal vision focuses on "saving lives", "energy innovation", and "improving eduction".

On a whole, Bill Gates' life and accomplishments will do more to create lasting change in this world than many other figures in our age. We could even consider that he will usher in a new epoch of technology and global progress.
